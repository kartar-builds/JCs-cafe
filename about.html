Who We Are

Our Story

The Concept

Where Two Worlds Beautifully Collide

Café JC's was born from a simple but ambitious idea — to create a space in Chandigarh that felt genuinely different. Not just another café, but a destination. A place where classic European café culture — unhurried, warm and beautiful — would meet the colour and depth of India's multi-cuisine dining scene.

Nestled inside the Coal Depot Complex at Sector 10D, the café was designed from the ground up to be a visual and culinary experience. Teal French doors, rustic wooden tables, trailing indoor plants, cozy book corners, and a lush garden patio — every element was chosen with intention.

"Food tastes better in beautiful places."

We believe a great meal is about far more than the plate. It's the light, the music, the company — and most importantly, the feeling that someone truly cared when they prepared your food.

The Space

Indoor Coziness Meets Garden Bliss

Step inside and you'll find a warmly lit interior dressed in wood, teal and warm amber tones. A curated book corner invites you to slow down, and soft background music sets the perfect tone.

Walk through the French doors and the garden opens up — a lush, airy outdoor patio where light filters through the leaves and the city feels a world away. Perfect for brunches, date evenings or lazy Sunday afternoons.

And as the sun sets, the candle-lit garden transforms into something truly special.

Our Journey

How Café JC's Came to Be

2017

The Doors Open

Café JC's opens at Coal Depot Complex, Sector 10D with a small but passionate team and a dream to create Chandigarh's most beautiful dining space.

2018

Pet-Friendly Goes Official

After popular demand and many unofficial furry visitors, we officially become one of Chandigarh's first pet-friendly cafés.

2019

Garden Expansion

The lush outdoor patio is expanded with new seating, garden lighting and a dedicated candle-lit evening setting.

2022

Menu Reimagined

We expand our menu to include Pan-Asian dishes and a full artisanal beverage programme, cementing our multi-cuisine identity.

Today

Still Growing

Café JC's continues to be one of Chandigarh's most loved and reviewed café destinations — and we're just getting started.

Our Philosophy

Food. Space. Memory.

We don't just make food. We curate experiences. From the moment you step through our teal French doors, every sensory detail is intentional — the aroma of freshly ground coffee, the warmth of wooden textures, the sound of quiet music under an open sky.

Our kitchen draws from four rich culinary traditions — North Indian, Italian, Continental and Pan-Asian — ensuring that every guest finds something to love, every single time.
